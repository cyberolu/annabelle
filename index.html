Biography

Annabelle Fasuba is a -year-old British sprinter specialising in 60m, 100m and 200m. In 2025, she became the national U15 champion across all three events‚Äîwinning the indoor 60m in 7.53s, the outdoor 100m in 11.51s (breaking the previous record of 11.56s), and equalling the U15 national 200m record with a time of 23.72s.
