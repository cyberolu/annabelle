🏁 Annabelle Opens Outdoor Season

5 April 2026 • Plymouth

Season Overview

Annabelle Fasuba opens her outdoor season on 5 April in Plymouth, marking the start of a new phase following a strong indoor campaign. While she will not compete in her main sprint events at this meet, the focus is on building momentum and preparing for the months ahead.

She will take on the 300m and, for the first time in competition, the Long Jump. These events are part of a broader approach to develop strength, improve coordination, and sharpen overall performance ahead of her return to sprinting.

Return to Sprint Events

Two weeks later, Annabelle returns to her core events, competing in the 100m and 200m at the Ken Trickey Memorial Open in Exeter. This will mark the true start of her outdoor sprint season.

Focus for the Season

The priority remains clear. Build confidence early, stay consistent in execution, and carry that form into the key competitions later in the season.

The journey continues.
