🏁 Outdoor Season Begins

5 April 2026 • Plymouth

+

Annabelle opens her outdoor season with a 300m and her first ever Long Jump...

Season Overview

Annabelle Fasuba will officially open her outdoor season on 5th April in Plymouth. While she won’t be competing in her main sprint events on this occasion, she will take on the 300m and step into new territory with her first ever Long Jump competition.

This early-season outing is all about exploration and preparation. It provides a valuable opportunity to shake off any early nerves, build confidence, and continue developing the technical work she has been refining during the indoor season.

Return to Sprint Events

Two weeks later, Annabelle will return to her core events, competing in the 100m and 200m at the Ken Trickey Memorial Open in Exeter. This will mark the true start of her sprint campaign for the season.

Focus for the Season

While there is plenty of excitement around her return, the focus remains clear. Manage early-season jitters, build on strong indoor performances, and translate training into competitive execution.

The journey continues.
